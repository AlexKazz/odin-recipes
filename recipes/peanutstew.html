Peanuts + Stew = Peanut Stew

"A Peanut Stew Makes Peanut Poo" - American Fecal Association

Ya like peanuts? Ya like thick stews? Well you've come to the right place.

Peanuts and stews can actually be combined. Here's what you'll need:

Ingredients

  • Peanuts
  • Peanut Butter
  • Stew

These are the steps you need to take to make this thick, saucy treat:

Steps

  1. Get the stew all hot and bothered until it's practically begging for peanuts
  2. Once you've teased it enough, throw those peanuts in. Also peanut butter
  3. Put it in a tupperware container so you can eat it wherever and whenever you get those peanut cravings
